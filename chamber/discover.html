The Lekki Free Zone

The Lekki Free Zone is a specially dedicated area of about 50 square kilometers where foreign investors are can invest and can repatriate 100% of their profits. The zone has easy access to the Lekki sea port and the proposed new Lekki International Airport.

Lekki Conservation Center

The Lekki Conservation Center is an oasis of natural habitat for different species of animals within the heart of Lagos. Visitors can view different species of animals in their natural habital especially the adventurous mona monkeys. If you are brave, you can climb the over 200 feet tree canopy walk.
