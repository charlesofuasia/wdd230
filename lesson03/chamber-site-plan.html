Lekki Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

The name of the website is lekkichamberofcommerce. It is tied to the name Lekki which is the locale of the chamber.

Purpose

The Lekki Chamber of Commerce represents all the leading businesses that operate around the Lekki district in Lagos State, Nigeria and offers networking opportunities for its members. This is the first and only point of call when in search of reputable businesses to transact business with, to this end, the chamber maintains a register of only reputable and trusted businesses. The chamber also organizes events to increase awareness and offers leadership training for members.

Scenarios

Visitors may visit the site for any of the following:

  • Search the directory for information on businesses in the Lekki
  • Search for information on how to become a member
  • Members that want to pay membership dues
  • Register for an event
  • Get information on upcoming events.

Personas

The potential site visitors will include local and foreign businesses seeking for information on businesses in the area, persons seeking information on upcoming events, members seeking to pay membership dues, and persons searching for information about lekki area.
